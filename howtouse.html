How to use

STEP 1: Go to registration page and create an account if you have no account.

STEP 2: Go to login page and login via your credentials

STEP 3: After successfull login. Click on vote section in menu which is in top bar

STEP 4: Select the candidate for vote from list.

STEP 5: Once you select click on save vote button for casting your vote

STEP 6: Now you done all step and the selected candidate shown in your dashboard
